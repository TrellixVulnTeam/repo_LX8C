Some Random Anime Facts:
The highest grossing anime film of all time is Spirited Away. Bringing a total of $275 million world wide Sazae-san is the longest running anime. Created in 1969, the show has 6430+ episodes. Sally the Witch, created in 1966 was the first of the "Magical Girl" anime genre.
"Over 9000!" was a translation error. The power level was actually " Over 8000!" Dragon Ball premiered in Weekly Shonen Jump 33 years ago. Char's Counterattack premiered 29 years ago
Code Geass is sponsored by the Japanese branch of Pizza Hut. Despite the fact that the series is set in an alternate reality, Pizza Hut is seen ordered many times. Kamehameha means "Turtle Destruction Wave." The creator of Cryaon Shin Chan (Yoshito Usui) died from falling off a cliff while trying to take a photo.
Animated Japanese films and television shows account for 60% of the world's animation-based entertainment. Astro Boy was the 1st anime to be broadcast outside Japan
Attack on Titan is based on a city named "Nordligen" in Germany. Warner Brothers has bought the rights to make a live action Bleach Movie.
Many surnames of the characters in 'Neon Genesis Evangelion' are named after Japanese WWII warships.
